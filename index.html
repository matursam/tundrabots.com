About

FTC Robotics Resource is a project for new teams created by FTC team 7083, the Tundrabots. After 4 years in FTC, we felt that it was time share all of the knowledge we've acquired with new teams. We hope that this site is a useful resource for teams that are just starting out.

Feel free to email our team at tundrabots@gmail.com with feedback or questions.

What is FTC?

FIRST Tech Challenge is a robotics competition for high schoolers, designed to be a “varsity sport for the mind”. FTC focuses on creating a team-based, sports-like competition for teenagers interested in STEM fields. As a part of the competition, teams are exposed to mechanical, electrical, and software engineering, as well as marketing and teamwork.
For more information, check out FIRST’s official website, which has many resources explaining all aspects of the competition.

Competition Day

When you are at the competition there is an ideal code of conduct that you should try to follow. Courteousness to other teams is of the utmost importance. The nicer you are to other teams, the nicer they will be to you. When a team needs a part or aid repairing their robot, do not hesitate to offer a helping hand. If you are being considered for an award, the judges may be keeping an eye on your team to determine if you truly deserve it. Have a few team members go around and talk to every team that you can, keeping track of their strengths and weaknesses to help you pick a team if you are an alliance captain. The competitions are fun and exciting events, and we wish you luck!
